About Asutosh College
        Asutosh College, located in Kolkata, West Bengal, is a prestigious undergraduate college that has been a cornerstone of education since its establishment in 1916. Named after the eminent educationist Sir Asutosh Mukherjee, the college holds a prominent position in the academic landscape of Kolkata. Affiliated with the renowned University of Calcutta, Asutosh College offers a diverse range of courses in arts, science, and commerce, catering to the educational needs and aspirations of students.
Courses Offered
We offer courses in the following departments:
Physics
The Physics Department at Asutosh College is a prominent academic department that offers comprehensive education and research opportunities in the field of physics. Located in Asutosh College, Kolkata, the department is dedicated to providing a strong foundation in theoretical and experimental physics.
Statistics
The Statistics Department at Asutosh College is a dedicated academic department that focuses on the study and application of statistical methods and techniques. The department offers a comprehensive education in statistics to equip students with the necessary skills for data analysis, interpretation, and decision-making.
Computer Science
The Computer Science Department at Asutosh College is a dynamic and thriving academic department that focuses on the study and application of computer science principles and technologies. Located in Asutosh College, Kolkata, the department offers a comprehensive curriculum to equip students with the necessary skills for a career in the rapidly evolving field of computer science.
Chemistry
The Chemistry Department at Asutosh College is a prominent academic department that focuses on the study and exploration of chemical principles, reactions, and their applications. Located in Asutosh College, Kolkata, the department offers a comprehensive curriculum that encompasses various branches of chemistry.
Microbiology
The Microbiology Department at Asutosh College encourages research and provides students with opportunities to engage in research projects, fieldwork, and industrial training. It emphasizes practical applications of microbiology in various fields such as healthcare, agriculture, biotechnology, and environmental science.
